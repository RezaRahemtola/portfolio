Solva is a Venmo-like payment app powered by Web3 technologies, empowering anyone to receive and transfer funds,
instantly & without fees, without knowing anything about crypto.

Built in 36 hours during the [ETHGlobal Taipei 2025 hackathon](https://ethglobal.com/showcase/solva-map8t), it received
3 prizes, totalling $5500 and was selected among the 8 finalist projects out of 226 by a jury of industry experts.
